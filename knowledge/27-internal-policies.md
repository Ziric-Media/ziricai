# Internal Policies
> Category: internal-policies | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: What is acceptable use?
**A:** Use ZiricAI for lawful customer communication — no spam, hate, or illegal content.

## Q: Can staff use personal WhatsApp for business?
**A:** Policy discourages personal numbers — use ZiricAI Business API for POPIA and CRM.

## Q: Who approves knowledge uploads?
**A:** Designated admin reviews pricing and policy documents before publish.

## Q: How often review knowledge?
**A:** Monthly review minimum; immediate update when prices or policies change.

## Q: Escalation policy for angry customers?
**A:** Negative sentiment triggers manager notification within 15 minutes.

## Q: Marketing message policy?
**A:** Marketing only to opted-in contacts with clear unsubscribe on WhatsApp.

## Q: Password policy for portal?
**A:** Strong passwords required; MFA recommended for admins.

## Q: Offboarding staff access?
**A:** Remove portal users same day as employment ends.

## Q: Incident response policy?
**A:** Report suspected breaches to security@ziricai.com within 4 hours.

## Q: Data retention policy?
**A:** Delete stale CRM records per schedule documented in privacy policy.

## Q: Refund authority policy?
**A:** Only managers approve refunds — AI shares policy text not decisions.

## Q: Brand voice policy?
**A:** Use approved tone guide — friendly professional unless industry pack specifies otherwise.

## Q: Document naming policy?
**A:** Prefix docs with category — Pricing-, Policy-, FAQ- for easier RAG retrieval.

## Q: Testing policy before go-live?
**A:** Run 10 test questions in sandbox before activating customer-facing AI.

## Q: Holiday coverage policy?
**A:** AI employees active 24/7; human on-call roster for escalations only.
