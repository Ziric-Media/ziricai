# Automation
> Category: automation | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: What is the Automation Builder?
**A:** A visual tool to create workflows: triggers, conditions, AI actions, CRM updates, emails, and webhooks.

## Q: What triggers are available?
**A:** New message, CRM stage change, tag added, form submit, schedule, and API events.

## Q: Can WhatsApp messages trigger workflows?
**A:** Yes — incoming WhatsApp is the most common trigger for lead capture automations.

## Q: What actions can workflows perform?
**A:** Send messages, assign CRM tasks, update fields, notify staff, call webhooks, and pause AI.

## Q: Do industry packs include workflows?
**A:** Each Marketplace pack ships starter automations you can edit.

## Q: How many workflows on Trial?
**A:** Trial includes 2 workflows and 50 workflow runs per month.

## Q: How many on Business?
**A:** Business includes unlimited workflows and unlimited workflow runs.

## Q: Can Sarah create automations?
**A:** Sarah can propose simple flows and open the builder for advanced editing.

## Q: Can I test workflows safely?
**A:** Use test mode and sandbox conversations before enabling production triggers.

## Q: What is a workflow run?
**A:** One execution of a workflow from trigger through all actions counts as one run.

## Q: Can workflows branch on conditions?
**A:** Yes — if/else on tags, intent, business hours, or custom CRM fields.

## Q: Can workflows send email?
**A:** Send notification emails to staff or customers when rules match.

## Q: Can workflows integrate externally?
**A:** Webhook actions POST JSON to your ERP, accounting, or custom systems.

## Q: Are there automation templates?
**A:** Marketplace packs and template gallery provide copy-paste starting points.

## Q: Can I delay actions?
**A:** Add wait steps — e.g., follow up 24 hours after no customer reply.

## Q: Can workflows assign team members?
**A:** Round-robin or rule-based assignment to agents via CRM tasks.

## Q: What happens when runs exceed limits?
**A:** Workflows pause until next cycle or you upgrade — warnings appear in portal.

## Q: Can I clone workflows?
**A:** Duplicate and tweak for new branches or campaigns.

## Q: Do automations respect POPIA?
**A:** Include consent checks before marketing messages or data exports in flows.

## Q: Can I disable a workflow quickly?
**A:** Toggle off without deleting — useful during promotions or outages.

## Q: Can AI actions sit inside workflows?
**A:** Yes — let AI draft replies or summarize threads mid-workflow.

## Q: Are automation logs available?
**A:** View run history, failures, and retries in the Automation module.

## Q: Can schedules send broadcasts?
**A:** Time-based triggers start approved WhatsApp template campaigns to segments.

## Q: Do workflows work across channels?
**A:** Triggers from any connected channel can drive the same CRM and notify logic.

## Q: Best first automation to build?
**A:** New WhatsApp message → create CRM lead → notify sales — ships in most packs.

## Q: Can automations send WhatsApp templates?
**A:** Yes — workflow actions send approved Meta templates to CRM segments or individual contacts.

## Q: How do I automate lead assignment?
**A:** New message trigger → create CRM lead → round-robin assign task to sales team members.

## Q: Can automations update CRM fields?
**A:** Set field values automatically when keywords, tags, or stage changes match workflow conditions.

## Q: What is a wait step in automation?
**A:** Pause workflow for hours or days before next action — e.g., follow up 48 hours after no reply.

## Q: Can I automate birthday messages?
**A:** Schedule trigger on CRM date field sends approved birthday WhatsApp template to opted-in contacts.

## Q: How do automations handle errors?
**A:** Failed runs log in Automation module with error detail; configure retry or notify admin actions.

## Q: Can automations call external APIs?
**A:** Webhook actions POST JSON payloads to any HTTPS endpoint accepting your data format.

## Q: What is the most common automation?
**A:** New WhatsApp message → create CRM contact → notify assigned agent — ships in most industry packs.

## Q: Can automations pause AI mid-conversation?
**A:** Yes — handoff action pauses AI and assigns thread to human agent immediately.

## Q: How do I automate appointment reminders?
**A:** Calendar booking trigger → wait 24 hours → send WhatsApp reminder template before appointment.

## Q: Can automations tag contacts automatically?
**A:** Intent detection or keyword match adds CRM tags for segmentation and reporting.

## Q: What triggers work on CRM stage change?
**A:** Moving deal to Won stage can trigger thank-you message and internal notification email.

## Q: Can I automate invoice follow-ups?
**A:** Collections pack workflow: overdue tag → wait 3 days → send payment reminder template.

## Q: How do automations respect opt-out?
**A:** Check CRM marketing consent field before any promotional workflow action executes.

## Q: Can automations create multiple tasks?
**A:** One trigger can create tasks for sales, finance, and manager roles in parallel branches.

## Q: What is automation test mode?
**A:** Run workflow against test contact without affecting production CRM or sending live messages.

## Q: Can automations send SMS fallback?
**A:** If WhatsApp session closed, SMS action sends notification where SMS integration configured.

## Q: How do I automate after-hours routing?
**A:** Business hours condition routes to AI during closed hours and notifies on-call agent for urgent keywords.

## Q: Can automations sync to Google Sheets?
**A:** Webhook POSTs lead data to Google Apps Script endpoint or Zapier-compatible URL.

## Q: What is workflow versioning?
**A:** Edit workflows live; consider duplicating before major changes to preserve rollback copy.

## Q: Can automations score leads?
**A:** Update lead score field based on keyword matches like 'budget approved' or 'ready to buy'.

## Q: How do I automate welcome sequences?
**A:** New CRM contact → send welcome WhatsApp → wait 2 days → send FAQ link message.

## Q: Can automations notify via Slack?
**A:** Webhook action POSTs to Slack incoming webhook URL for team channel notifications.

## Q: What limits apply on Starter automations?
**A:** Starter includes 5 workflows and 200 workflow runs per month.

## Q: What limits apply on Professional automations?
**A:** Professional includes 20 workflows and 1,000 workflow runs per month.

## Q: Can automations branch on sentiment?
**A:** Negative sentiment condition triggers manager alert while positive sentiment triggers review request.

## Q: How do I automate re-engagement?
**A:** Inactive 30-day CRM tag → send re-engagement WhatsApp template to opted-in segment.
