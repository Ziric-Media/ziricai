## Q: Can I pay with PayFast or Stripe?

**A:** Yes. **PayFast** is available for South African customers and supports card, EFT, and instant EFT payment methods familiar to local businesses. **Stripe** is supported for international card payments where available. The default payment method for monthly subscriptions is credit/debit card through the Company Portal under Billing → Payment Method.

Both PayFast and Stripe are PCI-compliant — ZiricAI never stores your full card number. Enterprise customers on annual contracts may alternatively pay via EFT or invoice. Contact billing@ziricai.com if you need help selecting the right payment method for your account.

## Q: When am I first charged after the free trial?

**A:** You are **not charged during the 14-day free trial** — no credit card is required at signup. Your first charge occurs when you **actively subscribe** to a paid plan (Starter, Professional, Business, or Enterprise) by adding a payment method and confirming your plan selection in Billing.

If you subscribe during trial (e.g., on day 5), billing starts immediately and you receive the full limits of your chosen tier. If you wait until trial expiry, subscribe before or on day 14 to avoid workspace pause. Upgrades during an active paid period are prorated for the remaining days in your billing cycle.

## Q: What happens to my account if I don't subscribe after trial?

**A:** If you do not subscribe after the 14-day trial, your workspace **pauses** — AI employees stop responding to customer messages, but your CRM data, knowledge documents, and configurations are **retained for a grace period**.

You can reactivate anytime by subscribing to a paid plan — your setup carries over without re-uploading content. Email reminders are sent before and after trial expiry. Export your data from CRM and Knowledge Base before closure if you decide not to continue. Contact support@ziricai.com if you need a brief extension while deciding.

## Q: Can I upgrade to a paid plan during my trial?

**A:** Yes — upgrading during your free trial is recommended if you are seeing results and approaching trial limits (100 conversations, one AI employee).

Go to **Billing → Change Plan**, select Starter (R999.99), Professional (R2,999), or Business (R4,999), add your payment method, and confirm. Your new plan limits apply **immediately** — more AI employees, conversations, users, and storage. All knowledge, CRM records, and configurations carry over seamlessly. You do not lose remaining trial days; billing starts from the upgrade date.

## Q: Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

**A:** Monthly ZiricAI subscriptions have **no minimum commitment** — cancel anytime from Billing with access until period end. Annual contracts for Professional, Business, or Enterprise are optional and arranged through sales@ziricai.com, typically as 12-month terms with discounted pricing. Self-serve customers are never locked into annual billing unless they explicitly choose it.
