# Integrations
> Category: integrations | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: What integrations does ZiricAI support?
**A:** WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram DMs, Telegram, web chat, email, SMS, Google Calendar, Microsoft 365, Stripe, and Paystack.

## Q: How do I connect Facebook?
**A:** Integrations → Facebook → authorize Meta Business Page for Messenger routing.

## Q: How do I connect Instagram?
**A:** Link Instagram Professional account through Meta Business Suite for DM routing.

## Q: Does Telegram work?
**A:** Connect Telegram bot token in Integrations for unified inbox threads.

## Q: How does email integration work?
**A:** Forward or connect support inbox so emails appear alongside chat threads.

## Q: Is SMS supported?
**A:** SMS gateway integration for outbound notifications and two-way where provider allows.

## Q: How do I connect Google Calendar?
**A:** OAuth connect calendar so AI employees offer real availability slots.

## Q: Does Microsoft 365 work?
**A:** Calendar and email integrations for Outlook-centric businesses.

## Q: How do I connect Stripe?
**A:** Add Stripe keys for payment links in Sales workflows and automations.

## Q: How do I connect Paystack?
**A:** Paystack supports ZAR payments popular with South African customers.

## Q: Can I use webhooks?
**A:** Automation webhook actions send events to ERP, accounting, or custom apps.

## Q: Is there a Zapier integration?
**A:** Use webhooks and API for Zapier-style connections; native Zapier on roadmap.

## Q: Can integrations fail safely?
**A:** Failed webhook runs log errors and retry per automation settings.

## Q: Do all plans include integrations?
**A:** Core channel integrations on all plans; API volume scales by tier.

## Q: Can Sarah help connect integrations?
**A:** Sarah launches connection wizards for WhatsApp, calendar, and payments step by step.

## Q: Are OAuth tokens encrypted?
**A:** Credentials stored encrypted with AES-256 at rest on Firebase.

## Q: Can I disconnect an integration?
**A:** Revoke anytime in Settings; historical conversations remain in CRM.

## Q: Does Instagram share inbox with Facebook?
**A:** Meta-linked channels route to the same unified inbox with channel labels.

## Q: Can Stripe subscriptions bill my customers?
**A:** Stripe handles your customer payments; ZiricAI billing is separate via portal.

## Q: Does Paystack support Instant EFT?
**A:** Paystack payment links support cards and local methods you enable in Paystack.

## Q: Can calendar block public holidays?
**A:** Upload holiday calendar in knowledge or block days in Google Calendar.

## Q: Are integration logs audited?
**A:** Connection events appear in admin audit trail for security reviews.

## Q: Can I integrate my ERP?
**A:** Webhook plus API export contacts and orders to SAP, Oracle, or custom ERP.

## Q: Do integrations need developers?
**A:** Standard OAuth integrations need no code; custom ERP may need webhook setup.

## Q: What if an integration token expires?
**A:** Portal prompts re-authentication; automations pause until refreshed.
