## Q: How much does ZiricAI cost?

**A:** ZiricAI pricing is transparent and billed in South African Rand (ZAR). Every new account starts with a **14-day free trial** at no cost and no credit card required.

Paid plans begin at **R999.99/month** (Starter), then **R2,999/month** (Professional — our most popular plan), **R4,999/month** (Business), and **Enterprise** with custom pricing for large organizations. All paid tiers include WhatsApp and web chat channels, a built-in CRM, analytics dashboards, Sarah as your internal operating assistant, and a Knowledge Base for grounded AI answers.

Pricing scales with your usage: AI employees, monthly conversations, outbound messages, team seats, storage, and automation capacity all increase at higher tiers. Most small and mid-sized businesses find the **Professional plan at R2,999/month** delivers the best balance of five AI employees, 5,000 conversations, and ten team users. There are no hidden setup fees on standard plans, and you can cancel monthly subscriptions at any time.
