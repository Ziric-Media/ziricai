# Security
> Category: security | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: How secure is ZiricAI?
**A:** TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest on Firebase, tenant isolation, and RBAC.

## Q: Where is data hosted?
**A:** Google Firebase cloud infrastructure with regional options for Enterprise.

## Q: Is data encrypted?
**A:** All customer data encrypted at rest and in transit.

## Q: Are tenants isolated?
**A:** Each company tenant has separate data paths — no cross-tenant leakage.

## Q: What authentication is used?
**A:** Secure login with password policies and optional MFA on Business+.

## Q: Are audit logs available?
**A:** Admin and integration changes logged for compliance reviews.

## Q: Who can access my data?
**A:** Only your authorized users and ZiricAI support when you grant access.

## Q: Does ZiricAI train public models on my data?
**A:** Your knowledge and conversations are not used to train public models.

## Q: How are API keys protected?
**A:** Hashed storage, rotation, and scoped permissions.

## Q: Is penetration testing performed?
**A:** Regular security assessments and dependency patching.

## Q: What about employee access?
**A:** ZiricAI staff access follows least-privilege and is logged.

## Q: Can I enforce IP restrictions?
**A:** Enterprise IP allowlists for portal and API.

## Q: How are backups handled?
**A:** Firebase backup and disaster recovery procedures.

## Q: What if I suspect a breach?
**A:** Contact security@ziricai.com immediately; incident response procedures apply.

## Q: Is MFA available?
**A:** Enable MFA for admin accounts in Security settings.

## Q: Are passwords hashed?
**A:** Industry-standard hashing for stored credentials.

## Q: How long are sessions valid?
**A:** Configurable session timeout for portal users.

## Q: Is file upload scanned?
**A:** Upload validation and size limits reduce malware risk.

## Q: Does ZiricAI support SSO?
**A:** Enterprise SAML/OIDC single sign-on available.

## Q: Are third-party integrations secure?
**A:** OAuth tokens encrypted; review connected app permissions regularly.

## Q: What compliance certifications apply?
**A:** POPIA and GDPR tooling; SOC-aligned practices on Enterprise.

## Q: Can I get a security questionnaire?
**A:** Enterprise prospects receive standard security pack on request.

## Q: How is WhatsApp data secured?
**A:** Processed securely; stored with same encryption as other channels.

## Q: Are deletion requests honored?
**A:** Account deletion workflows remove data subject to legal retention.

## Q: Where is the security contact?
**A:** security@ziricai.com for vulnerability reports and security inquiries.
