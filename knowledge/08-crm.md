# CRM
> Category: crm | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: Does ZiricAI include CRM?
**A:** Yes — built-in CRM with profiles, timelines, tags, tasks, notes, and pipelines.

## Q: How are leads captured?
**A:** Automatically from WhatsApp, web chat, social, email, and SMS conversations.

## Q: What is a customer timeline?
**A:** Chronological view of every message, note, task, and stage change per contact.

## Q: Can I customize pipeline stages?
**A:** Edit stages per industry pack or build custom pipelines for your sales process.

## Q: What is lead scoring?
**A:** AI assigns scores from intent keywords, engagement, and sentiment for prioritization.

## Q: Can I add manual notes?
**A:** Agents add private or shared notes visible on the customer timeline.

## Q: Can I assign tasks?
**A:** Create follow-up tasks with due dates and owners from inbox or CRM.

## Q: Does CRM sync with WhatsApp?
**A:** WhatsApp numbers map to profiles — returning customers continue existing threads.

## Q: Can I import contacts?
**A:** CSV import plus automatic creation from new conversations.

## Q: Can I export CRM data?
**A:** Export contacts and activity for reporting or migration.

## Q: Are custom fields supported?
**A:** Add fields for budget, location, product interest, and industry-specific data.

## Q: Can I segment customers?
**A:** Filter by tags, stage, channel, and last activity for campaigns.

## Q: Does CRM show sentiment?
**A:** Sentiment indicators highlight at-risk or delighted customers.

## Q: Can managers see team performance?
**A:** Dashboards show leads handled, response times, and conversion by agent.

## Q: Is CRM included on Trial?
**A:** Yes — full CRM on trial with limits on users and conversation volume.

## Q: Can CRM trigger automations?
**A:** Stage changes and tag events fire workflows automatically.

## Q: Does CRM support B2B accounts?
**A:** Link multiple contacts to an organization record on Business plans.

## Q: Can I merge duplicate contacts?
**A:** Merge duplicates to keep one timeline per customer.

## Q: Are GDPR erasure requests supported?
**A:** Delete or anonymize contact records per privacy policy workflows.

## Q: Can CRM track deal value?
**A:** Add deal amounts and expected close dates on pipeline cards.

## Q: Does AI update CRM automatically?
**A:** AI tags intent and updates fields based on conversation content.

## Q: Can I restrict CRM access?
**A:** Role permissions limit who views or edits sensitive customer data.

## Q: Is there a mobile CRM view?
**A:** Responsive portal works on phones for field agents.

## Q: Can CRM log call outcomes?
**A:** Log manual call notes and outcomes on the timeline after human follow-up.

## Q: How does CRM help Central Motors?
**A:** Every after-hours WhatsApp enquiry becomes a scored lead for sales consultants next morning.
