# Billing
> Category: billing | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: How am I billed?
**A:** Monthly in ZAR via card in portal; Enterprise may use EFT on annual contracts.

## Q: When does billing start?
**A:** After 14-day free trial ends unless you upgrade earlier.

## Q: Is VAT included?
**A:** Invoices show VAT per South African tax rules on your account settings.

## Q: Can I change plans?
**A:** Upgrade anytime prorated; downgrade at next renewal cycle.

## Q: What happens if payment fails?
**A:** Grace period with email reminders before service pause.

## Q: Can I get invoices?
**A:** Download PDF invoices from Billing → History.

## Q: Is there an annual option?
**A:** Contact sales for annual Professional, Business, and Enterprise pricing.

## Q: Can I cancel anytime?
**A:** Yes — cancel in portal; access until period end.

## Q: Do unused messages roll over?
**A:** Monthly limits reset each billing cycle — no rollover on standard plans.

## Q: What currency is charged?
**A:** South African Rand (ZAR) for all standard accounts.

## Q: How do I update card details?
**A:** Billing → Payment method → update card securely.

## Q: Are there setup fees?
**A:** No setup fees on standard plans.

## Q: Can I get a quote for Enterprise?
**A:** Contact sales for custom quote with SLA and unlimited usage.

## Q: Does trial require credit card?
**A:** No credit card required for 14-day trial.

## Q: What plan is most popular?
**A:** Professional at R2,999/month — 5 AI employees and 10 users.

## Q: Can NGOs get discounts?
**A:** Contact sales for NPO pricing on eligible plans.

## Q: How do I see current usage?
**A:** Billing dashboard shows messages, tokens, conversations vs plan limits.

## Q: What triggers upgrade prompts?
**A:** Approaching 80% of conversations, messages, or AI employee limits.

## Q: Can I add users mid-cycle?
**A:** Add users within plan cap; upgrade if you exceed user limit.

## Q: Is billing data secure?
**A:** Payments processed via PCI-compliant provider — ZiricAI never stores full card numbers.

## Q: Can accountant access invoices?
**A:** Forward PDF invoices or add billing contact email in settings.

## Q: What is proration?
**A:** Upgrade charges difference for remaining days in current cycle.

## Q: Do you offer refunds?
**A:** Refunds per fair use policy — contact support within 14 days of charge disputes.

## Q: Can I pause billing?
**A:** Contact support for exceptional circumstances — no self-serve pause on monthly.

## Q: Where is billing support?
**A:** billing@ziricai.com or live chat for invoice questions.
