# POPIA
> Category: popia | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: Is ZiricAI POPIA compliant?
**A:** Platform includes consent capture, access controls, audit logs, and data export tools aligned with POPIA.

## Q: What is POPIA?
**A:** Protection of Personal Information Act — South Africa's data privacy law governing personal information processing.

## Q: Who is the responsible party?
**A:** Your business is the responsible party; ZiricAI acts as operator processing data on your instructions.

## Q: How do I capture consent on WhatsApp?
**A:** First-interaction scripts collect opt-in before marketing or storing optional personal data.

## Q: Can customers request access?
**A:** Export contact records from CRM to fulfill access requests within statutory timelines.

## Q: Can customers request deletion?
**A:** Delete or anonymize records in CRM; retain only what law requires.

## Q: Where is my privacy policy?
**A:** Link your POPIA privacy policy in chat greetings and web widget.

## Q: Does ZiricAI process special personal information?
**A:** Avoid collecting health or biometric data unless legally justified and secured.

## Q: Are cross-border transfers allowed?
**A:** Firebase hosting may process outside SA — disclose in privacy policy and use Enterprise regional options.

## Q: What is a PAIA manual?
**A:** Your Promotion of Access to Information Act manual complements POPIA — link alongside privacy notice.

## Q: Do I need to register with the Information Regulator?
**A:** Certain operators must register — consult your legal advisor for your category.

## Q: How long can I retain data?
**A:** Set retention in policies; delete stale CRM records per your retention schedule.

## Q: Can AI employees share ID numbers?
**A:** Only when necessary and secured — minimize collection per POPIA minimality principle.

## Q: Are staff trained on POPIA?
**A:** Train portal users not to export personal data insecurely.

## Q: What if a data breach occurs?
**A:** Notify Information Regulator and affected data subjects as POPIA requires.

## Q: Does ZiricAI sign operator agreements?
**A:** Enterprise DPAs available defining processing scope and security.

## Q: Can minors' data be processed?
**A:** Obtain guardian consent for school and youth programmes.

## Q: Are marketing messages POPIA-compliant?
**A:** Send marketing only with opt-in and easy opt-out on WhatsApp and email.

## Q: How do I document processing?
**A:** Maintain Records of Processing Activities for your CRM and AI channels.

## Q: Does POPIA apply to my NGO?
**A:** Yes if you process personal information of donors, beneficiaries, or volunteers.
