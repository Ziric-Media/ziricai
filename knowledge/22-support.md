# Support
> Category: support | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: How do I contact support?
**A:** Email, live chat on marketing site, and in-portal help during trial and paid plans.

## Q: What are support hours?
**A:** Extended hours covering South African business times; Enterprise 24/7 options.

## Q: Does Sarah replace support?
**A:** Sarah handles how-to questions; human support handles billing and technical escalations.

## Q: Is onboarding support free?
**A:** Yes during trial and included with all paid plans.

## Q: How fast is email support?
**A:** Most tickets answered within one business day; urgent flagged in subject.

## Q: Is phone support available?
**A:** Priority phone for Enterprise; others use chat and email.

## Q: Where is the help centre?
**A:** Portal Help plus this Knowledge Base with 800+ answers.

## Q: Can I request a setup call?
**A:** Book onboarding call via support link after trial signup.

## Q: Do you help with WhatsApp verification?
**A:** Support guides Meta Business verification issues step by step.

## Q: Is there status page?
**A:** status.ziricai.com for platform incidents — subscribe for updates.

## Q: How do I report a bug?
**A:** Support ticket with steps to reproduce and screenshots.

## Q: Can I suggest features?
**A:** Feature requests via portal feedback reviewed monthly.

## Q: Is training available?
**A:** Webinars and tutorials plus Sarah for self-serve learning.

## Q: Do you support agencies?
**A:** Partner support channel for multi-tenant agency accounts on Enterprise.

## Q: What if I'm stuck on onboarding?
**A:** Ask Sarah in portal or open chat — average setup under 10 minutes with help.

## Q: Can support access my account?
**A:** Only with your permission for troubleshooting — logged in audit trail.

## Q: Is support included on Starter?
**A:** Yes — email and chat on all plans; Enterprise adds dedicated manager.

## Q: Where is community help?
**A:** Future community forum planned — Knowledge Base and support primary today.

## Q: How do I escalate urgent outage?
**A:** Mark ticket critical or email support@ziricai.com with OUTAGE subject.

## Q: Does support help with POPIA?
**A:** Guidance on platform tools — legal advice from your attorney.
