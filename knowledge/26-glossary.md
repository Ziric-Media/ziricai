# Glossary
> Category: glossary | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: What is an AI Employee?
**A:** A role-based AI agent (Reception, Sales, Support) answering on assigned channels from your Knowledge Base.

## Q: What is the Knowledge Base?
**A:** Repository of FAQs, policies, PDFs, and manuals your AI retrieves answers from.

## Q: What is RAG?
**A:** Retrieval-Augmented Generation — AI searches your documents first, then composes grounded replies.

## Q: What is CRM?
**A:** Customer Relationship Management — profiles, timelines, pipelines, and tasks in ZiricAI.

## Q: What is Sarah?
**A:** ZiricAI's AI Operating Assistant helping you configure the platform inside Company Portal.

## Q: What is the Marketplace?
**A:** Catalogue of 50+ industry packs with pre-built AI roles and workflows.

## Q: What is an industry pack?
**A:** Installable bundle of AI templates, knowledge seeds, CRM stages, and automations for one sector.

## Q: What is unified inbox?
**A:** Single view of WhatsApp, web, social, email, and SMS conversations.

## Q: What is a workflow?
**A:** Automation sequence triggered by events like new messages or CRM stage changes.

## Q: What is a workflow run?
**A:** One complete execution of a workflow from trigger through actions.

## Q: What is lead scoring?
**A:** Numeric priority assigned from intent, engagement, and sentiment signals.

## Q: What is POPIA?
**A:** Protection of Personal Information Act — South Africa's data privacy law.

## Q: What is GDPR?
**A:** General Data Protection Regulation — EU/UK personal data protection framework.

## Q: What is Meta Business API?
**A:** Official WhatsApp and Facebook messaging interface businesses use via platforms like ZiricAI.

## Q: What is a session window?
**A:** 24-hour WhatsApp period allowing free-form replies after customer message.

## Q: What is a WhatsApp template?
**A:** Pre-approved Meta message format for notifications outside session window.

## Q: What is tenant isolation?
**A:** Security model separating each company's data from others on shared infrastructure.

## Q: What is RBAC?
**A:** Role-Based Access Control — permissions by owner, admin, agent, viewer roles.

## Q: What is AES-256?
**A:** Advanced encryption standard used for data at rest on Firebase.

## Q: What is TLS 1.3?
**A:** Transport encryption protocol securing data between users and ZiricAI.

## Q: What is Firebase?
**A:** Google cloud platform hosting ZiricAI application and data storage.

## Q: What is intent tagging?
**A:** AI classification of customer goal — pricing, booking, complaint, etc.

## Q: What is sentiment analysis?
**A:** AI estimate of customer mood — positive, neutral, or negative.

## Q: What is handoff?
**A:** Transfer from AI to human agent in unified inbox.

## Q: What is Company Portal?
**A:** Web application where you manage ZiricAI for your business.

## Q: What is a token?
**A:** Unit measuring AI language model processing behind replies.

## Q: What is a conversation?
**A:** Distinct customer thread across one or more messages on any channel.

## Q: What is Stripe?
**A:** Payment gateway for card processing in Sales workflows.

## Q: What is Paystack?
**A:** African payment gateway popular in South Africa for cards and local methods.

## Q: What is Enterprise SLA?
**A:** Service Level Agreement with uptime and support commitments on custom plans.
