## Q: How long does setup take?

**A:** Most businesses complete ZiricAI's initial setup in **under 10 minutes** and can begin handling customer enquiries the same day.

The core setup — creating your workspace, choosing an industry pack, uploading your first FAQ document, and activating one AI employee — takes only a few minutes with Sarah's guided onboarding. Connecting **web chat** is instant. **WhatsApp** requires Meta Business verification, which may take one to three business days depending on Meta's review — but you can configure knowledge, CRM, and test AI responses via web chat while verification is pending.

Businesses with extensive knowledge libraries or multiple AI employees typically spend one to two hours over the first week refining content — not on technical configuration. No developers or IT staff are required for standard setup.

## Q: How do I get started with ZiricAI?

**A:** Getting started with ZiricAI follows a simple path from signup to live AI employee in minutes.

**Step 1 — Sign up:** Visit ziricai.com and click **Start Free Trial**. Enter your business name, email, and password. No credit card required. **Step 2 — Choose your industry:** Select an industry pack from the Marketplace (automotive, healthcare, schools, etc.) or start blank. Industry packs pre-load relevant FAQs and workflows. **Step 3 — Upload knowledge:** Add your top customer FAQs — a PDF, DOCX, or paste directly into the Knowledge Base editor. Start with 5–10 documents your staff answers daily. **Step 4 — Connect channels:** Link WhatsApp via the guided wizard, or enable web chat immediately. **Step 5 — Activate:** Turn on your first AI employee and send a test message. Sarah guides each step inside the Company Portal.

## Q: What is the onboarding wizard?

**A:** The ZiricAI onboarding wizard is a guided, step-by-step setup flow that takes new accounts from signup to a live AI employee without technical expertise.

The wizard walks you through seven stages: **account creation** (business name, email, password), **industry selection** (choose a Marketplace pack or custom), **WhatsApp connection** (Meta Business Manager linking and phone verification — skippable initially), **knowledge upload** (add FAQs, policies, and product documents), **AI training** (link documents to your AI employee and set tone), **test conversation** (send a sandbox message to verify answers), and **completion** (go live on connected channels).

Progress is saved at each step — you can pause and return later. Sarah also provides contextual help during onboarding if you get stuck. The wizard is available on trial and all paid plans at no extra cost.

## Q: What are the steps to go live?

**A:** Going live on ZiricAI follows a proven checklist that most businesses complete in one session.

**1. Create workspace** — Sign up and complete the onboarding wizard. **2. Install industry pack** — Choose a Marketplace pack matching your sector for pre-built FAQs and workflows. **3. Upload core knowledge** — Add your top 5–15 customer FAQs, price list, and business hours. **4. Configure AI employee** — Set name, role (reception/sales/support), greeting message, and business hours. **5. Connect WhatsApp** — Complete Meta Business verification and link your business number. **6. Test thoroughly** — Ask 10 real customer questions in the sandbox; verify answers match your documents. **7. Activate and promote** — Turn on live mode and add your WhatsApp number to your website, Google Business Profile, and marketing materials.

Do not promote your WhatsApp number until step 6 passes — accurate first impressions build customer trust.

## Q: Do I need technical skills to set up ZiricAI?

**A:** **No technical skills are required** for standard ZiricAI setup. The platform is designed for business owners, office managers, and customer service teams — not developers.

The onboarding wizard, Sarah's guided assistance, and industry packs handle configuration through point-and-click interfaces. Uploading knowledge is as simple as dragging a PDF or typing FAQs. Connecting WhatsApp uses a guided QR scan and Meta verification flow — no code, webhooks, or server setup.

Developers are only needed if you want **custom API integrations**, bespoke webhook automations, or white-label deployments — all optional. If you can use WhatsApp Business and upload a document, you can set up ZiricAI. Our support team and Sarah are available if you get stuck at any step.

## Q: What do I need before starting setup?

**A:** Before starting ZiricAI setup, gather a few items to make onboarding smooth and fast.

**Required:** A business email address for your account, your company name, and at least one FAQ document (PDF, DOCX, or TXT) covering your most common customer questions — pricing, hours, services, and contact details. **Recommended for WhatsApp:** A **WhatsApp Business** phone number not currently linked to another API provider, access to **Meta Business Manager** (free to create at business.facebook.com), and your business registration details for Meta verification. **Helpful but optional:** Your logo for branding, a price list or product catalogue, and a list of CRM pipeline stages you use today.

You can start without WhatsApp — web chat works immediately — and add WhatsApp once Meta verification completes. Industry packs reduce preparation by pre-loading sector-specific FAQs.

## Q: How do I set up WhatsApp on ZiricAI?

**A:** Connecting WhatsApp to ZiricAI uses the official Meta Business API through a guided setup wizard in the Company Portal.

**Step 1:** Go to Settings → Channels → WhatsApp → Connect. **Step 2:** Sign in to or create a **Meta Business Manager** account at business.facebook.com. **Step 3:** Register your business phone number — it must not be actively linked to WhatsApp on another API provider. **Step 4:** Complete Meta's business verification (typically one to three business days; you can continue configuring knowledge while waiting). **Step 5:** Scan the QR code or follow the embedded signup flow to link your number. **Step 6:** Send a test message from your personal phone to verify the AI employee responds correctly.

Sarah walks you through each step with contextual tips. While verification is pending, enable web chat to test AI responses immediately. See Module 10 (WhatsApp) for troubleshooting verification delays.

## Q: How do I set up multiple AI employees?

**A:** Setting up multiple AI employees lets you assign specialized roles — reception, sales, support — each with its own knowledge and channels.

**Step 1:** Confirm your plan supports the number of AI employees you need — Starter includes 1, Professional includes 5, Business includes 10, Enterprise unlimited. **Step 2:** Go to Agents → Create AI Employee. **Step 3:** Define each employee's name, role, greeting, and business hours. **Step 4:** Create or assign a dedicated Knowledge Base per employee — sales gets product catalogues, support gets troubleshooting FAQs. **Step 5:** Route channels appropriately: one WhatsApp number can serve multiple employees using intent detection, or assign different channels to different roles. **Step 6:** Test each employee independently before going live.

Best practice: start with one AI employee during trial, then split into Sales and Support on Professional when answer quality improves by separating knowledge domains.

## Q: How do I train the knowledge base?

**A:** Training your ZiricAI knowledge base means uploading accurate business content and linking it to the right AI employees — the AI learns exclusively from your documents, not the open internet.

**Start with essentials:** Upload your top 10 customer FAQs, business hours, price list, service descriptions, and policies as PDF, DOCX, or TXT files — or paste directly into the FAQ editor. **Link to AI employees:** In Agents → select employee → Knowledge → attach relevant documents. Sales employees get product info; support employees get troubleshooting guides. **Test and refine:** Ask real customer questions in the sandbox. If an answer is wrong or missing, add or update the source document — never edit AI behavior directly. **Iterate weekly:** Review the Analytics dashboard for "unknown questions" and add new FAQ entries from real conversations.

Date-stamp filenames (Pricing-2026-07.pdf) and retire outdated versions promptly. Accurate knowledge is the single biggest factor in AI answer quality.

## Q: What are common setup mistakes to avoid?

**A:** Avoid these common ZiricAI setup mistakes to go live faster with accurate AI responses.

**1. Promoting WhatsApp before testing** — Always send 10+ test messages and verify answers before sharing your number publicly. **2. Uploading outdated documents** — Stale pricing or old policies cause wrong answers; date-stamp files and remove retired versions. **3. One knowledge base for everything** — Mixing sales and support content confuses AI; split by employee role on Professional plans. **4. Skipping business hours** — Without hours configured, AI may respond outside your intended availability; set per channel and employee. **5. Ignoring Meta verification** — WhatsApp cannot go live until Meta approves your business; start verification on day one of trial. **6. No human handoff plan** — Configure escalation keywords ('manager', 'complaint', 'refund') and ensure agents enable mobile notifications.

Review the Analytics "unknown questions" report weekly during your first month to catch gaps early.

## Q: How do I set up web chat?

**A:** Web chat is the fastest ZiricAI channel to set up — it works immediately without Meta verification and is ideal for testing during your free trial.

Go to Settings → Channels → Web Chat → Enable. Customize the chat widget with your brand colours, logo, and welcome message under Company Settings → Branding. Copy the embed snippet and paste it into your website's HTML before the closing `</body>` tag, or use the WordPress/plugin integration if available. The widget connects to your active AI employee and Knowledge Base automatically.

Web chat shares the same unified inbox and CRM as WhatsApp — conversations, leads, and analytics appear alongside other channels. Many businesses enable web chat on day one of trial while WhatsApp verification is pending, then add WhatsApp when Meta approval completes.

## Q: How do I set up my team on ZiricAI?

**A:** Setting up your team ensures the right people have portal access to manage conversations, CRM, and AI employees.

Go to **Settings → Team → Invite Member**. Enter each person's email and assign a role: **Owner** (full access including billing), **Admin** (manage AI employees, knowledge, integrations — no billing), or **Agent** (inbox, CRM, conversation takeover — no configuration changes). Invited users receive an email to create their password and access the Company Portal.

Check your plan's user limit before inviting: Trial allows 1 user, Starter 2, Professional 10, Business 50, Enterprise unlimited. Enable MFA for Owner accounts as a security best practice. Train new agents with a 30-minute portal tour covering inbox takeover, CRM, and escalation — tutorial content is in Help → Tutorials.
