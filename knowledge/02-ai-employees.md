# AI Employees
> Category: ai-employees | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: What is an AI Employee?
**A:** An AI Employee is a dedicated agent with a name, role (Reception, Sales, Support), channels, and linked Knowledge Base that answers using only your content.

## Q: What is Reception AI?
**A:** Reception AI greets customers, answers FAQs, routes enquiries, captures contact details, and books appointments on your front-line channels.

## Q: What is Sales AI?
**A:** Sales AI qualifies leads, shares product info from your catalogue, sends quotes within approved rules, and pushes hot prospects to your CRM pipeline.

## Q: What is Support AI?
**A:** Support AI handles order status, troubleshooting steps, returns policy, and ticket creation while escalating complex cases to humans.

## Q: How many AI employees can I run?
**A:** Trial and Starter include 1; Professional includes 5; Business includes 10; Enterprise is unlimited.

## Q: Can each AI employee have different knowledge?
**A:** Yes. Link separate Knowledge Bases so Sales uses catalogues while Support uses policy documents.

## Q: Can AI employees share one inbox?
**A:** Multiple AI employees can serve different channels or departments while your team sees all threads in one inbox.

## Q: How do I train an AI Employee?
**A:** Upload FAQs, policies, PDFs, and product sheets to its Knowledge Base — no model training or coding required.

## Q: Does training require technical skills?
**A:** No. Drag-and-drop uploads and Sarah-guided wizards handle knowledge setup for non-technical owners.

## Q: How long until an AI Employee is accurate?
**A:** Most teams see useful answers after uploading 5–15 core documents; accuracy improves as you add content.

## Q: Can I set tone and personality?
**A:** Configure formal, friendly, or industry-specific tone presets plus custom greeting and sign-off messages.

## Q: Can AI employees hand off to humans?
**A:** Yes. Set rules to pause AI and notify staff when sentiment drops, keywords appear, or customers request a person.

## Q: What channels can one AI Employee use?
**A:** Assign WhatsApp, web chat, Facebook, Instagram, Telegram, email, or SMS per employee.

## Q: Can I pause an AI Employee?
**A:** Toggle active/inactive anytime from the portal without losing configuration or history.

## Q: Do AI employees work after hours?
**A:** Yes — they reply 24/7 including weekends and public holidays when your office is closed.

## Q: What languages can AI employees speak?
**A:** English, Afrikaans, and other SA languages when configured with multilingual knowledge content.

## Q: Can AI employees send media?
**A:** Share approved images, PDFs, and links from knowledge or templates on WhatsApp and web chat.

## Q: Are AI employees industry-specific?
**A:** Install Marketplace packs to get pre-tuned roles, scripts, and workflows for your sector.

## Q: What limits apply to AI messages?
**A:** Each plan caps monthly AI messages and tokens — see Pricing for Trial through Enterprise limits.

## Q: Can AI employees create CRM records?
**A:** Every new conversation can auto-create or update profiles with tags, intent, and lead scores.

## Q: Can AI employees run workflows?
**A:** Yes. Triggers like new messages fire automations — CRM updates, emails, tasks, and webhooks.

## Q: How fast do AI employees reply?
**A:** Typical first response is under two seconds once knowledge is loaded and channels are connected.

## Q: Can I duplicate an AI Employee?
**A:** Clone configuration, knowledge links, and channel settings to launch a second role quickly.

## Q: Who sees AI Employee performance?
**A:** Analytics shows volume, resolution rate, and handoffs per employee in the dashboard.

## Q: Can AI employees collect POPIA consent?
**A:** Yes — consent prompts and opt-in flows can run before storing personal information.

## Q: What happens at plan limits?
**A:** You receive usage warnings; upgrade or wait for the next billing cycle when limits are reached.

## Q: Can Sales and Support share CRM data?
**A:** Yes. All employees write to the same tenant CRM with shared customer timelines.

## Q: Can I assign AI to one WhatsApp number only?
**A:** Route one number to Reception AI and use automations to transfer threads internally.

## Q: Do AI employees hallucinate prices?
**A:** RAG grounding reduces guesses — always upload official price lists and policy documents.

## Q: Can AI employees schedule callbacks?
**A:** Collect preferred times and create CRM tasks assigned to your team for follow-up.

## Q: What roles exist beyond Reception, Sales, Support?
**A:** Industry packs may add Admissions AI, Booking AI, Intake AI, or custom role labels.

## Q: Can AI employees tag conversations?
**A:** Auto-tag intent (pricing, booking, complaint) for reporting and automation triggers.

## Q: How do I test before going live?
**A:** Use sandbox preview in the portal to ask test questions against your Knowledge Base.

## Q: Can AI employees refuse off-topic questions?
**A:** Configure scope rules so the AI stays on business topics and declines unrelated requests.

## Q: Do AI employees learn from chats automatically?
**A:** Suggested knowledge gaps appear for admin review — nothing publishes without your approval.

## Q: Can I set business hours per AI Employee?
**A:** Different hours per role — e.g., Sales 08:00–17:00, Support 24/7.

## Q: What avatar options exist?
**A:** Choose preset avatars or upload brand-aligned images for web chat and portal display.

## Q: Can AI employees upsell?
**A:** Sales workflows can suggest add-ons from approved upsell scripts in your knowledge.

## Q: How are AI employees billed?
**A:** Plan limits on AI employee count, messages, and tokens apply across all active employees.

## Q: Can I rename an AI Employee later?
**A:** Update display name anytime; customer-facing introductions use the new name on next message.

## Q: Do AI employees support group WhatsApp?
**A:** Focus is business API one-to-one and broadcast templates; group chat support varies by Meta policy.

## Q: Can AI employees verify identity?
**A:** Collect reference numbers or ID last digits per your scripts before sharing sensitive account info.

## Q: What is the difference from Sarah?
**A:** Sarah operates your business inside the portal; customer-facing AI employees serve your clients externally.

## Q: Can AI employees send WhatsApp templates?
**A:** Use approved Meta templates for outbound notifications where session windows require it.

## Q: How do I improve weak answers?
**A:** Add missing FAQs to Knowledge Base, refine wording, and mark incorrect replies for review.

## Q: Can AI employees handle complaints?
**A:** Support AI follows escalation scripts, logs sentiment, and notifies managers for urgent cases.

## Q: Are conversation transcripts stored per employee?
**A:** Yes — filter history and analytics by AI Employee role in the portal.

## Q: Can I restrict AI from giving legal advice?
**A:** Scope knowledge to informational FAQs and add disclaimers for regulated industries.

## Q: Do AI employees integrate with calendars?
**A:** Reception and Sales roles book appointments via Google Calendar or Microsoft 365.

## Q: Can one employee handle multiple locations?
**A:** Use location-aware knowledge documents or separate employees per branch for clarity.

## Q: What onboarding does Sarah provide for AI Employees?
**A:** Sarah creates employees, links knowledge, connects channels, and runs test conversations with you.

## Q: Can AI employees process refunds?
**A:** Follow policy docs only — escalate refund approvals to staff when rules require human sign-off.

## Q: How do I monitor live conversations?
**A:** Supervisors view the unified inbox in real time and can take over any thread.

## Q: Can AI employees work during load shedding?
**A:** Cloud-hosted AI stays online if your customers have connectivity — your portal needs internet too.
