# Pricing
> Category: pricing | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: How much does the Trial plan cost?
**A:** Trial is Free (14-day free trial, no credit card). All paid plans include WhatsApp, web chat, CRM, analytics, and Sarah as your operating assistant.

## Q: How many AI employees are included on Trial?
**A:** Trial includes 1 AI employee. Each has its own role, channels, and linked Knowledge Base.

## Q: What is the conversation limit on Trial?
**A:** Trial allows up to 100 conversations per billing cycle. A conversation is a distinct customer thread across any connected channel.

## Q: How many messages can I send on Trial?
**A:** Trial includes 500 outbound AI messages per month. Upgrade if your team consistently approaches this limit.

## Q: What token limit applies to Trial?
**A:** Trial includes 50,000 AI tokens monthly for language processing. Tokens measure model usage behind grounded replies.

## Q: How many team users on Trial?
**A:** Trial supports 1 portal user with role-based access for owners, admins, and agents.

## Q: What storage is included on Trial?
**A:** Trial includes 512 MB file storage for knowledge uploads, attachments, and media.

## Q: How many workflows on Trial?
**A:** Trial includes 2 automation workflows in the Automation Builder.

## Q: How many knowledge documents on Trial?
**A:** Trial allows 10 knowledge documents in your Knowledge Base library.

## Q: What workflow run limit on Trial?
**A:** Trial includes 50 workflow executions per month for triggers like new WhatsApp messages or CRM stage changes.

## Q: What API call limit on Trial?
**A:** Trial includes 1000 API calls monthly for integrations and custom apps connecting to ZiricAI.
