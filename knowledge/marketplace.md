# Marketplace
> Category: marketplace | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: What is the ZiricAI Marketplace?
**A:** A library of 50+ industry packs — pre-built AI employees, knowledge templates, workflows, and CRM stages you install in minutes.

## Q: How do I install a pack?
**A:** Open Marketplace in the portal, choose your sector, click Install, and Sarah completes configuration with you.

## Q: How long does installation take?
**A:** About four minutes for core setup; add your documents afterward for full accuracy.

## Q: Can I switch industry packs?
**A:** Install a new pack and migrate knowledge — contact support if replacing active production workflows.

## Q: Are Marketplace packs free?
**A:** Packs are included with your subscription; no separate pack purchase on standard plans.

## Q: Can I preview before installing?
**A:** Read pack descriptions, included roles, and sample workflows before installation.

## Q: Who maintains pack content?
**A:** ZiricAI updates platform templates; you maintain business-specific prices and policies.

## Q: Can I publish a custom pack?
**A:** Enterprise partners can discuss private pack publishing with our team.

## Q: Do packs include automations?
**A:** Yes — starter workflows like lead capture, booking confirmation, and escalation ship with each pack.

## Q: Can agencies use Marketplace for clients?
**A:** Agencies deploy packs per client tenant with isolated branding and knowledge.

## Q: What is the Automotive pack?
**A:** Dealership Sales and Reception roles with vehicle catalogue, finance FAQ, and test-drive booking templates.

## Q: What is the Restaurant pack?
**A:** Table booking, menu FAQ, and delivery info workflows for hospitality businesses.

## Q: What is the Healthcare pack?
**A:** Patient info and appointment routing with POPIA-aware consent messaging.

## Q: What is the Legal pack?
**A:** Matter intake, consultation booking, and practice area FAQ without giving legal advice.

## Q: What is the Schools pack?
**A:** Admissions FAQ, term calendar answers, and parent enquiry capture for education institutions.

## Q: Can I mix pack knowledge with my docs?
**A:** Yes — packs seed templates; your uploaded documents override and extend answers.

## Q: Are packs updated automatically?
**A:** Platform template updates deploy centrally; review release notes in Product Updates.

## Q: How do I find the right pack?
**A:** Filter by industry or ask Sarah to recommend a pack during onboarding.

## Q: Do packs support WhatsApp templates?
**A:** Many include suggested Meta template text you submit for approval in Business Manager.

## Q: Can I uninstall a pack?
**A:** Remove pack-linked templates carefully — retain custom knowledge you still need.

## Q: Is Central Motors using a pack?
**A:** Yes — the Automotive pack powers Central Motors demo and live dealership workflows.

## Q: Are new packs added regularly?
**A:** We ship new verticals and regional variants — see Product Updates and release notes.

## Q: Can Marketplace packs be white-labelled?
**A:** Enterprise arrangements can rebrand pack experiences for franchise networks.

## Q: Do packs include analytics views?
**A:** Industry dashboards and CRM reports align with each pack's typical KPIs.

## Q: What if my industry is missing?
**A:** Start with the nearest pack, customize knowledge, or request a new pack via support.
