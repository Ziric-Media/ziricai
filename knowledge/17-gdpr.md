# GDPR
> Category: gdpr | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: Does ZiricAI support GDPR?
**A:** Tools for consent, access, erasure, portability, and processing records support GDPR-aligned operations.

## Q: Who is the data controller?
**A:** Your business is controller for customer data; ZiricAI is processor when serving EU data subjects.

## Q: Can EU customers use ZiricAI?
**A:** Yes with appropriate DPA and privacy notices.

## Q: What is lawful basis for processing?
**A:** Typically legitimate interest or consent for customer service — document your basis.

## Q: How do erasure requests work?
**A:** Delete CRM records and conversation history subject to legal holds.

## Q: Is data portability supported?
**A:** Export CRM and conversation data in machine-readable CSV.

## Q: Are subprocessors listed?
**A:** Firebase and AI providers listed in DPA annex for Enterprise.

## Q: Where is EU data stored?
**A:** Enterprise can discuss EU region hosting requirements.

## Q: Do I need a DPO?
**A:** Appoint DPO if required by GDPR Article 37 for your organization.

## Q: How long is retention?
**A:** Configure retention matching GDPR storage limitation principle.

## Q: Can I cookie-consent on web chat?
**A:** Web widget supports privacy notice and consent before chat starts.

## Q: Are automated decisions covered?
**A:** AI assists humans; significant automated decisions may need GDPR safeguards.

## Q: What about Brexit UK GDPR?
**A:** Similar tools apply for UK data subjects with UK addendum on request.

## Q: Can I restrict processing?
**A:** Mark contacts restricted and pause automations per request.

## Q: How are international transfers handled?
**A:** Standard contractual clauses in Enterprise DPA.

## Q: Does GDPR apply to SA companies?
**A:** Applies when processing personal data of individuals in the EU/UK.

## Q: Are breach notifications supported?
**A:** Incident process coordinates 72-hour regulator notification where applicable.

## Q: Can children use the chat widget?
**A:** Avoid targeting children without parental consent mechanisms.

## Q: Is profiling used?
**A:** Lead scoring is lightweight — disclose in privacy policy.

## Q: Where is GDPR documentation?
**A:** Security and POPIA categories plus Enterprise DPA cover GDPR topics.
