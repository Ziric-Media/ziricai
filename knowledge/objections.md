# Objection Handling
> Category: objections | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: ZiricAI is too expensive.
**A:** Starter is R999.99/month — less than one missed sale or a fraction of receptionist salary. Central Motors recovered cost from after-hours leads in week one.

## Q: AI will replace my employees.
**A:** AI handles repetitive FAQs and nights/weekends; your team focuses on complex sales and relationships. Handoff keeps humans in control.

## Q: It won't work for my industry.
**A:** 50+ Marketplace packs cover schools, legal, healthcare, automotive, funeral, retail, and more — install in four minutes.

## Q: My customers want real people.
**A:** Offer instant AI first response plus easy human escalation — customers prefer fast answers over waiting hours.

## Q: We don't get enough enquiries.
**A:** Even a few after-hours WhatsApp messages per week can justify Starter if one converts monthly.

## Q: Setup will take too long.
**A:** Median go-live is under 10 minutes with Sarah — upload a handful of FAQs and scan WhatsApp QR.

## Q: I'm not technical.
**A:** No code required. Sarah guides every step in plain language.

## Q: WhatsApp is enough with manual replies.
**A:** Manual replies fail at night and during rush — AI never delays and logs every lead in CRM.

## Q: We tried chatbots before and failed.
**A:** Generic chatbots guess. ZiricAI answers only from your Knowledge Base with CRM and WhatsApp built in.

## Q: AI will say wrong prices.
**A:** Upload official price lists — RAG grounding prevents invented pricing when knowledge is complete.

**A:** Consent tools, encryption, and audit logs reduce POPIA risk versus informal WhatsApp on personal phones.
