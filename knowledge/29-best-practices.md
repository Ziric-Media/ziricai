# Best Practices
> Category: best-practices | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: Best first document to upload?
**A:** Top 10 customer FAQs your staff answers daily — immediate accuracy boost.

## Q: How many documents needed?
**A:** Start with 5–15 core docs; expand weekly from real customer questions.

## Q: Should AI disclose it's AI?
**A:** Many businesses introduce AI employee by name — transparency builds trust.

## Q: Review conversations how often?
**A:** Weekly review of unresolved threads and incorrect answers.

## Q: Optimize WhatsApp profile?
**A:** Complete business profile, hours, and catalog link before promoting number.

## Q: Train team on handoff?
**A:** Agents respond within 5 minutes when AI escalates — set mobile notifications.

## Q: Structure CRM stages?
**A:** Match stages to your sales process — don't use default if mismatched.

## Q: When to add second AI employee?
**A:** Split Sales and Support when one role knowledge confuses answers.

## Q: Marketing opt-in best practice?
**A:** Separate service messages from promos — POPIA opt-in for marketing.

## Q: Keep pricing current?
**A:** Update price list PDF same day as changes — stale prices erode trust.

## Q: Use analytics weekly?
**A:** Monday review: volume, conversion, sentiment, top unknown questions.

## Q: Automate first?
**A:** Lead capture notify — highest ROI automation for most SMEs.

## Q: Test after knowledge update?
**A:** Ask 3 questions from updated doc in sandbox before customers see changes.

## Q: Peak season preparation?
**A:** Upgrade plan before Black Friday or registration week — avoid limits.

## Q: Multilingual best practice?
**A:** Upload parallel FAQ docs per language rather than one mixed doc.

## Q: Escalation keywords?
**A:** Configure human handoff for 'manager', 'complaint', 'refund', ' lawyer'.

## Q: Avoid over-automation?
**A:** Don't auto-send marketing on every message — respect customer intent.

## Q: Central Motors lesson?
**A:** They prioritized after-hours WhatsApp — where 42% conversion gain came from.

## Q: Document version control?
**A:** Date-stamp filenames — Pricing-2026-03.pdf — retire old versions.

## Q: Onboard new staff?
**A:** 30-minute portal tour: inbox, CRM, takeover, tasks — use tutorial category.

## Q: Measure trial success?
**A:** Track leads captured day 3–14 — if zero, fix channel or knowledge not platform.

## Q: Industry pack tip?
**A:** Customize pack greetings with local branch name and owner sign-off.

## Q: Security hygiene?
**A:** Enable MFA for owners, rotate API keys quarterly, remove ex-staff users promptly.

## Q: Customer feedback loop?
**A:** Mark helpful/not helpful on AI replies where available — refine knowledge.

## Q: Plan upgrade signal?
**A:** Upgrade when consistently above 80% conversation or message limits two months running.
