# Technical Troubleshooting
> Category: troubleshooting | ZiricAI Platform Knowledge Base

## Q: WhatsApp not connecting?
**A:** Re-scan QR in Integrations, verify Meta Business verification complete, check phone online.

## Q: AI gives wrong answers?
**A:** Upload missing FAQs, check document linked to AI employee, remove outdated PDFs.

## Q: No messages in inbox?
**A:** Confirm channel connected green, webhook active, customer using correct business number.

## Q: Hit conversation limit?
**A:** Upgrade plan or wait for cycle reset — check Billing usage meters.

## Q: Workflow not firing?
**A:** Verify workflow enabled, trigger matches channel, conditions not blocking test message.

## Q: Cannot login?
**A:** Reset password, check email spam, clear browser cache, try incognito.

## Q: Upload failed?
**A:** Check file under size limit, supported format PDF/DOCX/TXT, stable internet.

## Q: CRM duplicate contacts?
**A:** Merge duplicates in CRM → Contacts → merge tool.

## Q: Template message rejected?
**A:** Edit template to Meta guidelines, resubmit in Business Manager, wait approval.

## Q: Slow AI responses?
**A:** Check Firebase status page, simplify long knowledge docs, reduce attachment size.

## Q: Payment failed?
**A:** Update card in Billing, ensure sufficient funds, contact bank for 3DS blocks.

## Q: Integration token expired?
**A:** Reconnect OAuth in Integrations — Google, Meta, Stripe, Paystack.

## Q: Web chat widget not showing?
**A:** Verify embed script on site, no ad blocker, HTTPS enabled.

## Q: Team member no access?
**A:** Owner invites user with correct role — check user limit on plan.

## Q: Analytics looks wrong?
**A:** Confirm timezone in settings, date filter includes test vs production traffic.

## Q: Sarah not responding?
**A:** Refresh portal, check internet, try different browser, report if outage on status page.

## Q: API 401 errors?
**A:** Rotate API key, verify Authorization header, check key not revoked.

## Q: API 429 rate limit?
**A:** Reduce call frequency or upgrade plan for higher API cap.

## Q: WhatsApp media not sending?
**A:** Check media size limits, format supported, session window open.

## Q: Calendar slots wrong?
**A:** Sync timezone on Google Calendar and portal business hours settings.

## Q: Automation email not sent?
**A:** Verify recipient email, spam folder, SMTP action configured in workflow.

## Q: Knowledge search empty?
**A:** Re-index by re-saving document, confirm linked to active AI employee.

## Q: Trial expired unexpectedly?
**A:** Trial is 14 days from signup — upgrade to continue or contact support.

## Q: Cannot upgrade plan?
**A:** Clear payment method error, contact billing if card valid but charge fails.

## Q: Facebook page disconnected?
**A:** Re-authorize Meta permissions when page admin password changed.
